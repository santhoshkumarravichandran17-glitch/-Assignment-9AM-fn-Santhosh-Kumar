AJITH KUMAR

‘Don’t Promote Me, Promote Motorsports’

Ajith Kumar sees racing as a form of therapy and a way to push personal limits, emphasizing that it requires intense focus, discipline, and teamwork, and gives a greater appreciation for life. He uses his racing to promote Indian cinema globally and to represent India on the international stage, a source of pride for him. He has formed a professional racing team, Ajith Kumar Racing, to foster interest in the sport within India and to compete at the highest levels of international motorsport.
