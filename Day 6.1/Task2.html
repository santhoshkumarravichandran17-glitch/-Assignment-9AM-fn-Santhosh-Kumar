Ajith Kumar

Ajith Kumar is a popular Indian film actor, motor racer, and philanthropist, primarily active in the Tamil film industry (Kollywood). He is widely known by his nickname “Thala”

Ajith Kumar Award's

He was conferred the Padma Bhushan (India’s third-highest civilian honour) for his contribution to the arts in April 2025. He has won numerous industry awards: Filmfare Awards South – 6 wins. Vijay Awards – 13 wins. Tamil Nadu State Film Awards – 6 wins. SIIMA (South Indian International Movie Awards) – 6 wins. Cinema Express Awards – 7 wins. Dinakaran Cinema Awards – 1 win.
