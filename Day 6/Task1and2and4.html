Leati Joseph Anoaʻi (Samoan pronunciation: [a.noˈaʔ.i] ah-no-AH ee; born May 25, 1985), better known by his ring name Roman Reigns, is an American professional wrestler, actor and former football player. As a wrestler, he has been signed to WWE since 2010, where he performs on the SmackDown brand. Regarded as one of the best professional wrestlers in the world,[9][10] Reigns' 1,316-day reign as WWE Universal Championship is the longest reign of the title's history, the fourth longest world title reign in WWE history and the longest championship reign recognised by the company since 1988
